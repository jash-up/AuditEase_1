About AuditEase

The professional audit document management platform built for modern finance and compliance teams.

What is AuditEase?

AuditEase is a comprehensive document management system designed specifically for audit firms, finance departments, and compliance professionals. It provides a secure, organized, and efficient way to store, track, and manage all types of financial and regulatory documents — from GST returns to audit reports and beyond.

Key Features

AES-256 Encryption

Every file stored in the vault is encrypted with AES-256-CBC before touching disk — military-grade security.

Version Tracking

Full version history for every document — never overwrite, always maintain a complete audit trail.

Approval Workflows

Assign approvers to documents. Track pending approvals and verification status in real-time.

Powerful Filtering

Find any document instantly with multi-dimensional filtering by category, status, month, year, and more.

Archive Management

Archive completed documents to keep your vault clean. Unarchive any time to resume editing.

Category Organization

Organized by Indirect Tax, Employees, and Income Tax with granular subcategories for each.

Dark Mode & Accessibility

AuditEase respects your system preference and provides a beautiful dark mode. Toggle anytime using the sun/moon button in the top bar. All colors are defined as CSS custom properties — never hardcoded — ensuring perfect contrast in both modes.
